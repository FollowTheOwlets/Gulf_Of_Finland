The Gulf of Finland is a great place for outdoor activities. Here you can meet a lone skier conquering the expanses of the snowy desert and active users of cheesecakes and sleds. The Gulf of Finland in winter is a place where you definitely need to go with your family.

Speedriding - skiing with a wing over your head is a very young sport. Speedriding is shrouded in an attractive halo of a prohibited sport, although in fact there is no such ban: winged skiers are forbidden to show themselves only on prepared trails. And yet lifeguards at most ski resorts look askance at speedriders. After all, this type of skating is considered so dangerous that even base jumpers call its adherents crazy.
